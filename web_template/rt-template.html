Route 1

Route 1 is the first route in Roria and has the lowest level Pokémon for the player to battle and catch. Wild Pokémon encountered are at Levels 2 to 4 — always lower than the player's Starter Pokémon.

This Route locate right next to Mitis Town - the first town player enter in the game.

Right before the player reaches the blue gate for Cheshma Town, Jake catches up in the nick of time hurriedly and suddenly calls the player back home...

Description

Upon leaving Mitis Town, the player is greeted by a small patch of grass, as well as Bug Catcher Ralph shortly after. After defeating him, 2 small trees which can be chopped down with HM1 Cut, obtained later on in Route 4, are seen at the far back, concealing a path leading to the TM98 Power-Up Punch. As the player turns left, larger patches of grass can be seen in front of Camper Jack as the path becomes wider. Ascend the short staircase to further progress and to battle Picnicker Susie. After eventually defeating her, the player can make an anti-clockwise U-turn to bypass the fence and pick up a bottle of Potion. Turning right from Susie leads the player through a final patch of grass on this route to the gate for Cheshma Town.

Trivia

- The blocked path containing TM98 Power-Up Punch was added during the free access update.

- With 4 out of the 6 Wild Pokémon species and Camper Jack's Zigzagoon giving Speed EV upon defeat, Route 1 is good for beginners to train their Pokémon's Speed EVs.

- However, once they progress further in the game, it is much more efficient to find Beach Babe Samantha on Battle Pier, Rosecove Beach or Camper Pal on Route 9.
